Famous Tupig in Currimao

Tupig is a delicacy that originated from the Northwestern Luzon region of Pangasinan, Tarlac, and Ilocos. It is usually made up of ground rice, sugar, gata and ginadgad na buko combined together and wrapped in banana leaves and cooked over live charcoal grill.
